Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information KHAN STRUCTURE ("we", "us", "our") collects when you visit khanstructure.com, request access to a subscription, or contact us via Telegram, and how that information is used.

1. Information we collect

  • Voluntary submissions — When you request access, you provide your TradingView username, Telegram username, optional email, and a USDT transaction hash for payment verification.
  • On-chain payment data — USDT (BEP20) transactions are public on the BNB Smart Chain. We verify them but do not store private wallet keys.
  • Basic site usage — Aggregated analytics (page views, language preference, anchor clicks). We do not use cross-site tracking cookies.

2. How we use it

  • Verify your subscription payment and grant access to TradingView invite scripts or the MT5 EA.
  • Provide support via Telegram and email when you contact us.
  • Improve the site experience based on aggregate analytics.

3. What we do not do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third-party data brokers.
  • We do not access your trading account, broker login, API keys, or wallet beyond verifying the payment transaction hash you submit.
  • We do not place tracking cookies that follow you across other sites.

4. Data retention

Subscription records (TradingView/Telegram username, TxID) are kept for the duration of your active subscription plus 12 months for accounting. After that, identifying information is deleted upon written request.

5. Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by messaging the official KHAN STRUCTURE Telegram support channel. We respond within 24 business hours.

6. Children's data

The service is intended for adults aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Material changes will be announced on the official Telegram channel.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us via the official KHAN STRUCTURE Telegram listed on the main site support section.
