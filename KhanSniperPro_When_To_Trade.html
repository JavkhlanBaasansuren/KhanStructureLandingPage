Indicator-ийн дохиог хэзээ авах, хэзээ алгасах вэ — практик гарын авлага · 2026-06-17

Бүх зүйл indicator өөрөө график дээр харуулдаг — энд зөвхөн "юуг нь хараад шийдэх вэ" гэдгийг эмхэлж өгөв.

Гол зарчим: дохио бол candle хаагдсаны дараа л хүчинтэй

KhanSniperPro-ийн LONG / SHORT шошго зөвхөн бар хаагдах үед баталгаажна (non-repainting).

⏳ Бар хаагдтал бүү яар. Бар дундуур гарч ирсэн дохио бар хаагдахдаа алга болж магадгүй. Хаагдсан барын дохиог л ажил болго.

5. ХЭЗЭЭ АРИЛЖАА ХИЙХГҮЙ ВЭ (алгасах нөхцөл)

❌ Дараах үед дохиог алгас — winrate-ийг чирэх магадлалтай:
  • ADX босгоосоо доош (OK биш) → тренд сул, chop

  • Stats дээр Win% бага + R < 1.0 хосолсон → энэ зах зээл/тохиргоо тааруу

6. Орсны дараа — менежмент (indicator өөрөө хийнэ)

  1. TP1–TP4 шугам entry-ээс зурагдана — хэсэгчлэн ашиг авах түвшингүүд
  2. TP1 хүрэхэд → SL breakeven руу шилжинэ (эрсдэлгүй болно)
  3. TP2 хүрэхэд → SL-ийг TP1 түвшинд lock (runner ашиг хамгаална)
  4. Trailing Stop (улаан тасархай) → эсрэг flip эсвэл SL хүртэл байрлана
  5. Эсрэг flip гарвал → позицийг хаах дохио
Гол дүрэм: SL-ийг гар аргаар бүү ухраа. Indicator-ийн trailing/lock логик алдагдлыг хязгаарлахаар зохиогдсон.

KHAN | Sniper Pro — When to Trade?

When to take a signal, when to skip it — practical guide · 2026-06-17

The indicator displays everything on the chart itself — this document organises "what to look at before deciding".

Core rule: a signal is only valid after the candle closes

KhanSniperPro's LONG / SHORT labels are confirmed only when the bar closes (non-repainting).

⏳ Don't act before the bar closes. A signal that appears mid-bar may disappear when the bar closes. Only act on a signal from a fully closed bar.

1. Signal sequence — "Get ready" → "LONG/SHORT"

SignalMeaningAction
▲ Get ready to long / shortPre-signal: price is approaching the trailPrepare — get your order ready, but DON'T ENTER
LONG / SHORT labelConfirmed flip entryEnter only now

"Get ready" is only an alert — it doesn't guarantee entry. ADX/HTF/Session filters may block the signal afterwards.

2. Pre-entry CHECKLIST

✅ Trend & Strength

  • Confirmed LONG/SHORT label present (bar closed)
  • ADX OK — Stats panel shows ADX value above threshold (not chop)
  • HTF trend aligned — same direction as 4H supertrend signal (auto-checked when HTF cut is On)

✅ Location

  • LONG in discount (lower half of ATR range), SHORT in premium (upper half)
  • Not stacked directly at a major macro Support/Resistance (LONG not directly below Macro R)

✅ Timing

  • Within London (07–16 UTC) or NY (12–21 UTC) session — high liquidity
  • Not Asia (00–07 UTC) session (choppy market, lower quality)
  • Not a News window (default: around 12:30 / 14:00 / 18:00 UTC)
  • Not after Friday 18:00 UTC (Friday cutoff auto-blocks)

5. WHEN NOT TO TRADE (skip conditions)

❌ Skip the signal in these cases — likely to drag down win rate:
  • ADX below threshold (OK not showing) → weak trend, chop
  • HTF in opposite direction → counter-trend flip
  • News window (12:30 / 14:00 / 18:00 UTC ±) → sharp spike, fake flip
  • Friday evening / before weekend (18:00 UTC+) → gap risk
  • Mid-Asia session or other low-liquidity periods
  • Entry stacked directly at a Macro R/S level
  • "Get ready" appeared but no confirmed LONG/SHORT → do not enter
  • Stats show low Win% + R < 1.0 together → poor market/settings combination

6. After entry — management (the indicator handles it)

  1. TP1–TP4 lines are drawn from entry — partial profit levels
  2. When TP1 is hit → SL moves to breakeven (risk-free)
  3. When TP2 is hit → SL locked at TP1 (protects runner profit)
  4. Trailing Stop (red dashed) → stays in place until counter-flip or SL hit
  5. Counter-flip → signal to close the position
Key rule: Never manually move the SL back. The indicator's trailing/lock logic is designed to limit losses.
